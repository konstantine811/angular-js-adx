Our mission

At ADx Healthcare, we seek to contribute to the scientific advancement of Alzheimer’s disease healthcare through collaborative technology offerings and research contributions. We also aim to provide individuals, families and healthcare providers with answers for Alzheimer’s disease through screenings, education, advocacy, and support.
