Lifestyle & other factors

Lifestyle and other factors can also influence the chances of developing late-onset Alzheimer's disease.

Consult with a healthcare professional before making any major lifestyle changes.

Family history

Parents, siblings, and children of an individual with late-onset Alzheimer's disease have a higher chance of developing the disease themselves.
