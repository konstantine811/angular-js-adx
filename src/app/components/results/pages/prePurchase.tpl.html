Alzheimers Genetic Test

The Alzheimer's ApoE Genetic Test detects which two copies of the ApoE gene you have. The ApoE gene is the strongest genetic risk factor for Alzheimer's disease. The ApoE gene comes in three forms called variants: ApoE-e2, ApoE-e3, and ApoE-e4.
